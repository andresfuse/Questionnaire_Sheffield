Welcome

Welcome to this data collection form for the estimation of functional forms within the framework of the project led by the University of Sheffield and Universidad de los Andes (Bogotá, Colombia). Here, you will be presented with a series of questions aimed at understanding more how mode preferences change when factors such as time, trips using active transport modes, car or bus, or journey enjoyment are considered. These factors have been identified at a workshop held at the University of Sheffield on 16th November 2025 and we are asking for your help to turn what we learnt into actual numbers. You will be asked to position sliders for 23 scenarios across 6 sections. The whole form takes approximately 8–12 minutes.
